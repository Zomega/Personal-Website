Simmons Hall Dashboard


Dashboard is a HTML5 Application displayed on multiple television screens throughout Simmons Hall at MIT. It's designed to provide residents with information they use on a daily basis. I did most of the front end styling for Dashboard using LESS and CoffeeScript. A development version of the site is here. Dashboard has a very specific deployment target, so little effort has been made to ensure browser compatibility.

MASLAB 2012


MASLAB (Mobile Autonomous Systems Laboratory) is MIT's most intense IAP robotics competition. Participants are asked to design, CAD, build, program, and test their robots from scratch in just over a month. Robots then compete in a ladder tournament. I served on a team of four freshmen at our mechanical systems engineer.

3D Scanner


During my Junior Year of high school, I became very interested in 3d Prototyping. As part of this interest, I set out to build something capable of scanning small objects. I devised a low-cost solution using only parts I had on hand, like an astronomy laser, a chemistry kit stirring rod, a web cam, and KNEX. I was able to reconstruct point clouds of several objects at a reasonable resolution.
